Miles Halter goes to a boarding school to get a new start. Once he's at school, at first is an outsider, but soon makes friends with several different people. He befriends a beautiful girl named Alaska and is completely mezmorized by her. He soon finds out that she is a lot more complex then she seems and gets engulfed in lies and mischief. Based on the best-selling book 'Looking For Alaska' by John Green.
